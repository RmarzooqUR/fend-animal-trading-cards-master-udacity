Royal Bengal Tiger

Tigers are conscious about the rights of females and kids.Hence, they wait for the females and kids to eat the kill first,unlike lions.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Size: Head and body, 5 to 6 ft; tail, 2 to 3 ft
  • Average life span: 8 to 10 years
  • Population Trend: Decreasing

The Bengal tiger is the most numerous tiger with more than 2,500 left in the wild, found primarily in the indian subcontinent
